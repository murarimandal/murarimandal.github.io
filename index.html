I am an Assistant Professor at the School of Computer Engineering, KIIT Bhubaneshwar. I lead the RespAI Lab where we work on the topics related to responsible AI, machine unlearning, data valuation, ethical use of large language models (LLMs) and diffusion models. My research work has been published in ICML, KDD, AAAI, ACM MM, CVPR. Check out my research group’s website here: RespAI Lab

Earlier, I was a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at National University of Singapore (NUS). I worked with Prof. Mohan Kankanhalli and Prof. Jussi Keppo in the School of Computing. Long time back, I graduated in 2011 with a Bachelors in Computer Science from BITS, Pilani.
